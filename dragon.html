Dragon Fruit Juice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dragon fruit (peeled and chopped)
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add the chopped dragon fruit to a blender.
  2. Pour in the cold water and blend until smooth.
  3. Strain the juice through a fine sieve to remove pulp (optional).
  4. Stir in lime juice and sweetener (if using).
  5. Pour the juice into glasses over ice cubes.
  6. Garnish with mint leaves and serve chilled.
