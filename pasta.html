Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200 grams pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (400 grams) crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes to the pan and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, drain and add it to the sauce. Toss well to combine.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Tips: You can add vegetables like spinach or bell peppers to the sauce for extra nutrition. Enjoy your pasta!
