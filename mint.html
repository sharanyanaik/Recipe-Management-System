Mint Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1â€“2 teaspoons sugar or honey (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash the fresh mint leaves thoroughly.
  2. Add the mint leaves and cold water to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Strain the mixture through a fine sieve to remove pulp (optional).
  5. Stir in the lemon juice and sweetener (sugar or honey).
  6. Pour the mint juice into glasses over ice cubes.
  7. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and serve chilled.
