200g digestive biscuits (crushed)
100g melted butter
250g cream cheese
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 cup blueberries
2 tbsp blueberry jam
Fresh blueberries (for garnish)
Mint leaves (optional)

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions

  1. Mix crushed biscuits with melted butter and press into the base of a springform pan. Chill for 30 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form, then fold into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Pour the mixture onto the crust and smooth the top. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. In a pan, heat blueberries and jam until soft. Let cool completely.
  6. Spread the topping over the chilled cheesecake.
  7. Garnish with fresh berries and mint leaves. Serve chilled!
