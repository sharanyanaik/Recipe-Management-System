Strawberry Juice

Ingredients

  • 4 cups strawberries (fresh or frozen, hulled)
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1-2 teaspoons sugar or honey (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add the hulled strawberries to a blender.
  2. Pour in the cold water and blend until smooth.
  3. Strain the juice through a fine sieve to remove any pulp (optional).
  4. Stir in the lemon juice and sugar or honey (if using).
  5. Pour the strawberry juice into glasses over ice cubes.
  6. Garnish with mint leaves and serve chilled.

Tips: Use ripe strawberries for the best flavor. You can also mix in other fruits like bananas or oranges for a unique twist!
