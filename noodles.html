Stir-Fried Veggie Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil (optional)
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan or wok, heat vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  4. Add bell pepper, carrot, and cabbage. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until tender but still crisp.
  5. Stir in soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper.
  6. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss everything together until well combined.
  7. Drizzle with sesame oil if using, and stir to coat.
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.

Tips: Use any veggies you like, such as broccoli or snap peas. Adjust spice level by adding more or less chili sauce.
