Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 2 cups diced tomatoes
  • 18-20 raw cashews
  • 2-3 tbsp fresh cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 200-250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1-2 green chiles (slit)
  • 1-inch ginger (julienned)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices and herbs

Instructions

  1. Soak the cashews in hot water for 20-30 minutes. Drain and blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Blend the diced tomatoes into a puree.
  3. In a pan, heat butter on low heat. Add a bay leaf and sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw aroma disappears.
  5. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the butter separates.
  6. Add the cashew paste and sauté for 3-4 minutes on low heat.
  7. Mix in 1 cup of water, bring to a simmer, and let the curry thicken.
  8. Add ginger julienne, green chiles, salt, and a pinch of sugar.
  9. Gently stir in the paneer cubes and cream. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  10. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.

Tips: Add more butter and cream for a richer taste. Adjust spice levels by varying the amount of chili powder and green chiles!
