🔥 Chicken Tikka

🧂 Ingredients

🍗500g Boneless Chicken
🥛1/2 cup Yogurt
🍋2 tbsp Lemon Juice
🧄1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
🌿1 tbsp Cumin Powder
🌿1 tbsp Coriander Powder
🌶️1 tbsp Garam Masala
🌶️1 tsp Red Chili Powder
🟡1 tsp Turmeric
🧂Salt to Taste
🛢️2 tbsp Oil
🌿Coriander Leaves
🧅Onion & Lemon Slices

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and spices into a marinade.
  2. Add chicken to the marinade, cover, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (overnight is best).
  3. Preheat grill/oven to 200°C (400°F).
  4. Thread chicken on skewers, brush with oil, and grill for 15–20 minutes until done.
  5. Garnish with coriander, onion rings, and lemon wedges.
💡 Marinate overnight for best flavor.
🧄 Serve with mint chutney and naan!
